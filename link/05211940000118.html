WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF POKEMON

Pokémon is short for “Pocket Monsters", the original Japanese name. The franchise has its roots in a gaming magazine in the early 1980s in Japan—Game Freak, started by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori.

Dragonite

Dragonite is a Dragon/Flying type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Dragon Pokémon.

Venusaur

Venusaur is a Grass/Poison type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Seed Pokémon.

Blastoise

Blastoise is a Water type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Shellfish Pokémon.

Mewtwo

Mewtwo is a Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Gen 1 and has the National Dex number #150. The Pokédex classifies it as the Genetic Pokémon.
